Professional Profile

Curriculum leadership grounded in strong English teaching

I am an experienced English Language and Literature educator and department leader focused on the intersection of rigorous instruction, inclusive practice, and long-term student growth.

My work spans grades 7 through 13 and supports schools working across IMYC, Cambridge Lower Secondary, IGCSE, and IBDP frameworks. I partner with teachers and school leaders to strengthen curriculum coherence, assessment quality, and classroom culture.

Current focus areas

  • English curriculum leadership and vertical alignment
  • Teacher coaching, moderation, and professional development
  • Inclusive instruction for neurodivergent and multilingual learners
  • Speaking and consultancy for schools seeking thoughtful academic leadership
Thought Leadership

Education ideas worth sharing in staff rooms and on stages

My writing and speaking center on a simple belief: language education should equip students to think critically, communicate with confidence, and participate fully in the world around them.

Through practical insight pieces, curriculum reflections, and leadership commentary, I share ideas designed for teachers, school leaders, and international school communities.
